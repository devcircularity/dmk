About Daima Mkenya

Daima Mkenya celebrates Kenyan heritage through vibrant Kikoy fabrics, sustainably sourced from local cotton farms in Busia County. Our mission is to empower communities, promote sustainable fashion, and connect designers with high-quality, locally produced fabrics. We believe in a circular economy where fashion drives positive impact – from supporting cotton farmers to inspiring designers to create beautiful, meaningful pieces. Each product we offer reflects this commitment to sustainability, craftsmanship, and community development.
